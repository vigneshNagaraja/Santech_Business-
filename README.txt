Fillable Weekly Timesheet (Auto-calc)
====================================
- Type directly into the sheet (dates/times/lunch). Totals & pay auto-calc.
- Save (local device) / Clear / Print to PDF.

Deploy steps (GitHub Pages):
1) Upload index.html + styles.css to your repo root.
2) Enable Pages (Deploy from branch: main, Folder: /(root)).
3) Share the Pages link on WhatsApp.

Use:
- TIME IN/OUT use 24h time inputs (08:00, 17:30). Lunch mins default 30.
- Rate per hour is optional; shows total pay if set.
- **Saved data stays on the device** (localStorage) only.
